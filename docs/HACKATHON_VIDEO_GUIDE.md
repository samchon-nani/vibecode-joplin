# Hackathon Video Guide for BillHarmony

## Video Type: Product Demo + Pitch Video

Based on your documentation (especially the **Irresistible Offer** and **Elevator Pitch** documents), you need to create a **demo video** that combines:
1. **Product demonstration** (showing the app in action)
2. **Pitch presentation** (explaining the value proposition)

## Recommended Video Structure

Follow the **4-Act Structure** from your Irresistible Offer document:

### **Act 1: Setup (Bigger Context)** - 30-45 seconds
**What to Show:**
- Hook: Start with a compelling problem statement
- Industry context: Healthcare billing is broken
- The shift: AI is changing what's possible

**Script Ideas:**
- "Healthcare billing is broken. Patients face opaque pricing, confusing bills, and financial stress..."
- "For years, providers, insurers, and patients have endured inefficiencies from opaque billing..."
- "Recent breakthroughs in AI have changed what's possible..."

**Visual:**
- Quick montage of pain points (confusing bills, stressed patients)
- Or start with your app logo/branding

---

### **Act 2: Confrontation (Breaking the Old Way)** - 20-30 seconds
**What to Show:**
- Current alternatives are broken
- Problems with existing solutions

**Script Ideas:**
- "Current solutions rely on manual processes, fragmented systems, and payor-specific software..."
- "These approaches waste time, cause errors, and leave patients in the dark..."

**Visual:**
- Show examples of confusing bills or complex processes
- Contrast with your solution

---

### **Act 3: Resolution (Demo the Better Way)** - 2-3 minutes ‚≠ê **MAIN FOCUS**
**What to Show:**
- **Live demo of your application**
- Walk through key features
- Show how it solves the problems

**Demo Flow (Recommended):**

1. **AI-Powered Search** (30-45 seconds)
   - Show the natural language search
   - Example: "I need an MRI with Blue Cross insurance in Beverly Hills, CA within 25 miles"
   - Show how AI understands the query
   - Highlight: "No more complex forms‚Äîjust describe what you need"

2. **Price Comparison Results** (30-45 seconds)
   - Show the results with hospitals
   - Highlight price transparency
   - Show in-network vs out-of-network
   - **Show inpatient vs outpatient classification:**
     - Point out the visual indicator on each result card
     - **Inpatient**: Requires hospital admission/overnight stay (e.g., surgeries, complex procedures)
     - **Outpatient**: Same-day procedures, no overnight stay (e.g., MRIs, X-rays, minor procedures)
     - Explain: "Each result clearly shows whether it's an inpatient or outpatient procedure, helping patients understand what to expect"
   - For inpatient procedures, mention typical length of stay (e.g., "typically 1-3 days" or "average stay: 2-5 days")
   - Show cost savings
   - **Future feature mention**: "Currently, we show the procedure classification. In the future, we're working on adding detailed cost breakdowns for inpatient stays, including room charges, medication costs, and extended care expenses‚Äîgiving patients even more transparency into their total financial responsibility."
   - Highlight: "Clear, transparent pricing for every option"

3. **Financial Navigation Hub** (30-45 seconds)
   - Show the different tabs (Price Comparison, Charity Eligibility, Financial Assistance)
   - Demo charity eligibility screening
   - Show how it helps patients find assistance
   - Highlight: "All financial navigation in one place"

4. **Key Features** (30 seconds)
   - Quick highlights:
     - HIPAA & CMS Compliant badges
     - AI-powered transparency
     - Proactive communication options
     - EPIC integration (if showing)

**Script Ideas:**
- "BillHarmony is an AI-powered platform that unifies billing data across all payor systems..."
- "No more manual entry or fragmented spreadsheets..."
- "Instantly adapts as rules change‚Äîzero expensive IT overhauls..."
- "Real-time transparency lets you handle issues before they become problems..."
- "Each result clearly shows whether it's an inpatient or outpatient procedure, and for inpatient services, you'll see the typical length of stay‚Äîhelping patients plan not just for cost, but for time as well."
- "Looking ahead, we're developing detailed cost breakdowns for inpatient stays, including room charges, medication costs, and extended care expenses, so patients have complete visibility into their total financial responsibility."

**Visual:**
- Screen recording of your app
- Smooth transitions between features
- Highlight key UI elements with annotations if needed

---

### **Act 4: Call to Action** - 20-30 seconds
**What to Show:**
- Early adopter program offer
- Next steps
- Strong closing

**Script Ideas:**
- "We're launching a limited early-adopter program..."
- "50% off for the first 6 months, with full access to all features..."
- "Schedule a discovery call to secure your spot..."

**Visual:**
- Show your contact info or CTA
- End with your logo and tagline: "BillHarmony - Bringing Clarity to Care Costs"

## Video Specifications

### **Length**
- **Total: 3-5 minutes** (most hackathons prefer 3-4 minutes)
- Keep it concise and engaging

### **Format**
- **Screen recording** for the demo portion
- **Voiceover** narration
- Optional: **Face cam** for intro/outro (adds personal touch)

### **Technical Requirements**
- **Resolution**: 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps minimum
- **Audio**: Clear voiceover, no background noise
- **File Format**: MP4 (most compatible)

## Video Production Tips

### **Before Recording:**
1. **Prepare your demo data:**
   - Use realistic examples (Beverly Hills, CA / 90210)
   - Test all features work smoothly
   - Have backup examples ready

2. **Write a script:**
   - Use your Irresistible Offer document as a guide
   - Practice the narration
   - Time yourself (aim for 3-4 minutes)

3. **Set up your screen:**
   - Close unnecessary apps
   - Use a clean browser/desktop
   - Zoom in on important UI elements
   - Use a cursor highlight tool if helpful

### **During Recording:**
1. **Speak clearly and confidently:**
   - Use the "Strong Delivery Tips" from your Irresistible Offer doc
   - Pause after key points
   - Show enthusiasm

2. **Show, don't just tell:**
   - Let the demo speak for itself
   - Point out features as you use them
   - Use annotations if needed

3. **Keep it smooth:**
   - Practice the demo flow
   - Edit out mistakes later
   - Use smooth transitions

### **After Recording:**
1. **Edit:**
   - Cut out mistakes and dead time
   - Add text overlays for key points
   - Add background music (subtle, professional)
   - Add transitions between sections

2. **Polish:**
   - Color correction if needed
   - Audio leveling
   - Add intro/outro graphics with your branding

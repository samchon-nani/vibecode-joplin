Branding Guide for BillHarmony
Brand Name
BillHarmony
Chosen to complement the existing product “MedHarmony,” emphasizing unity and clarity in
healthcare billing.
Tagline
"Bringing Clarity to Care Costs"
Conveys transparency and the brand's mission to simplify healthcare billing.
Brand Personality and Voice
Personality Traits: Trustworthy, Innovative, Clear, Understandable, Correct, Transparent,
Navigational
Voice Style: Professional yet approachable, Clear and concise, Empathetic, Credible, Supportive
Color Scheme
Primary Colors: Blue and Green
Accent Colors: Teal, Gold/Yellow
Neutrals: White/Light Gray
Logo
Design Elements: A harmony wave or connection nodes; modern sans-serif typography; inclusion
of AI elements.
Appearance: Balanced and symmetrical, adaptable for various mediums.
Marketing Strategy
Audience: Healthcare providers, payors, and billing departments.
Channels: Digital marketing, educational content, partnerships, testimonials, user-focused
experiences.
Focus: Highlight AI functionalities, transparency benefits, and ease of integration.
